Welcome to SkillMatrix!

Here developers of a company can enter and rate their skills. Each developer will enter a number of years and a self assessed rating of 1 star (something like: 'I have heard about it' to 5 stars (something like: 'I have done this for what seems like forever and I know everything about it').

Other people can search for skills and see which person has these skills.

This is a TEST environment! Data will NOT be saved and might be lost!
